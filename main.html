Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully plant an idea into someone’s mind.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio plays Dom Cobb, alongside Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Ken Watanabe, each portraying specialists in dream manipulation.
